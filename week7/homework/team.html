Our Team

Here you can match our faces to the voices you hear!

Jack

Jack has a Masters in Music History and has played the guitar since he was 10. His favorite band is Aerosmith and The Cure.

Brandon

Brandon has a background in working at concert venues and cross country tours, but his favorite thing to do is play the drums at 5am.

Dana

Dana has worked with the Chicago Philharmonic, playing the cello. She has also done some well known jingles throughout the years, such as McDonalds, Apple, Kroger, and more.
